You know when people say the best ideas come when you're in the shower? Well it's true. Because that's how this idea was born. I created an monogram using a 'c' and an 'a'. I also wanted to play off with both serif and san serif typefaces.

I believe the original was Futura and Adobe Garamond, but it has slightly changed over the years. I chose a pink color for the letter 'c' so your eye will first see that letter and chose a darker hue—maroon—for the 'a'. I was also obsessed with the color combination at the time.
