CHELSEA ALWAYS HAD A KNACK FOR MAKING AND CREATING NEW THINGS.

Her inqusitive nature always led her to new curiosities that eventually lead to the web. In high school she taught herself HTML and CSS and instantly fell in love with coding.

In Chelsea’s spare time she enjoys trying new flavors San Francisco has to offer, walking miles on end because Muni is never an option, and pretending to be a runner by running in races.

Chelsea was born and raised in Mililani, Hawaii.
