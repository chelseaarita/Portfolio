My name is Chelsea Arita

I am a graphic and visual designer based in San Francisco, California. I have freelanced and worked with a number of companies within the Bay Area such as Interbrand, Williams-Sonoma, Bare Escentuals, and the University of California, San Francisco.

I like taiko drumming (ancient Japanese percussion using large drums), sciences, and taking photos. I am originally from Hawaii and I studied graphic design at the Academy of Art University.
