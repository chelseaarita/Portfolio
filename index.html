CHELSEA ALWAYS HAD A KNACK FOR MAKING AND CREATING NEW THINGS.

Her inqusitive nature always led her to new curiosities that eventually lead to the web. In high school she taught herself HTML and a bit of CSS and instantly fell in love with coding.

Instead of majoring in web development, Chelsea studied print graphic design at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco, California. It took her nearly ten years to get back into the web and wondered why it took her so long.

Chelsea was born and raised in Hawaii. She also enjoys eating delicious foods, watching college football, and pretending to be a runner by running in races.
