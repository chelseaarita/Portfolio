CHELSEA ARITA    PEARSON FOUNDATION
Summary:
Hokulani Bake Shop is a bakery located on the Hawaiian Islands. They were founded in 2005 by Tushar and Ana Dubey and had won Food Network's "Cupcake Wars" in 2012.
Hokulani Bake Shop finds their inspiration through the culture and beauty of Hawaii. Their cookie and cupcake designs represent the Hawaiian lifestyle that include the marine life, nature, culture, and the aloha spirit.
Hokulani prides themselves creating cupcakes from scratch and contains 100% real butter, sugar, and no hydrogenated oils or corn syrups.

Problem:
Recreate the existing logo and website for Hokulani Bake shop.

Solution:
I wanted to recreate a design that best matched the personality Hokulani Bake Shop. The current logo and website was conservative, nostalgic, and not very memorable. I wanted to bring in a fresher, modern approach while still being conservative and preserving all the elements Hokulani embraced.
