Chelsea Arita is a graphic designer, print and web production artist, web designer and developer, and mobile designer based in San Francisco, California. She's freelanced and worked with a number of companies within the Bay Area such as Interbrand, Williams-Sonoma, Bare Escentuals, and the University of California, San Francisco.

Chelsea likes running, sweet bikes, and taking photos. (Not limited to Instagram.) She studied graphic design at the Academy of Art University and is originally from Hawaii.
