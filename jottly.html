Write, collaborate, and be social all at the same time. For this project I was asked to create a logo. I played off with a few ideas that included a semicolon that could've been seen as an abstract 'j', but went with a different approach. I played off with a pencil idea while thinking of going back to basics. I chose bright and poppy colors because I felt the original logo wasn't very lively and probably wouldn't attract too many users. I wanted to make sure my logo attracted users, mainly younger users to get them excited about writing.
