Twin Spires is an online wagering site and an extention of Churchill Downs and the Kentucky Derby. Players are now able to wager from all over the world in the comfort of their own homes without having to be present in a derby game. I was asked to create a logo for their website while still keeping the iconic spires. At the time of the design, I was really inspired by Michael Schwab's illustration and design and wanted to apporach this logo with that style. I kept the type with a simple san serif and kept it grey so it would match the look and feel of the logo as opposed to black that would have been too harsh.
