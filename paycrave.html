Paycrave is a mobile app for food trucks located in your city. I was given the logo and color scheme so my task was to create the UI for the login, map, truck details, recent transactions, purchases, and ratings. My first step was to make the app as user-friendly as possible, while still maintaining a clean design that was easy to please and follow.

The first thing you see when you click on the app is the login page (shown left). I made it straight forward and easy for customers to login with their social media accounts if they didn't want to log in directly into the app.
