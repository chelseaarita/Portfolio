The project was to create an exisiting or nonexisiting company with a skincare line. My skincare line was called "Kai", meaning 'ocean' in Hawaiian by Roxy Quiksilver. My concept was to have an upscale skincare line with having the bottles resemble surfboards, which is one of the sports Roxy Quiksilver tailor towards. I worked from sketches to execution. I used used rub down transfers on bottles, had my bottles professionally sprayed painted by a local company in San Francisco, and photographed by a local company in San Francisco.
